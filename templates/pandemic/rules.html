Rules


Play

Each player turn is divided into 3 parts:
  • Do 4 actions.
  • Draw 2 Player cards.
  • Infect cities.
    • After a player is done infecting cities,
    the player underneath goes next.     		Players should freely give each other advice.
    Let everyone offer opinions and ideas.
    However, the player whose turn it is decides what to do.
    Your hand can have City and Event cards in it.
    City cards are used in some actions and Event cards can be played at any time.
